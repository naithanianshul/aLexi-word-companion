Say hello to aLexi!

You could be on a journey to expand your vocabulary, maybe on the course of preparation for an exam like GRE, IELTS or TOEFL, or maybe because you simply want to improve your understanding of the English language. This application could be the thing that helps you efficiently achieve your goal.

aLexi will help you build your personal lexicon. You choose which words you want to add to your vocabulary.

This application helps you learn and revise words from your own personal wordlist. It will document every word's meaning, sentence example and word connotation. Whenever you decide to revise your words, you can search a word or let the app pop up a random word from your wordlist along with its the meaning, sentence example and related words. You can transverse the related words and refresh your mind map, or if you are looking for a challenge, use the Flash Cards to test your current memory!
